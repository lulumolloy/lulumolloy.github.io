Context

This poster explains a grammar rule of English which only needs to be taught to non-native speakers. Given that adjective order isn’t vital to clear communication (in English) I targeted the poster towards more advanced learners, while leaving room for younger ones. I chose a comic book-esque theme to appeal to the audience and draw attention. I used simple words to make the concept easier to grasp.

This project was done in two steps: First an essay researching the topic, and secondly the creating of an infographic based on the previous work. During the process I created a poll to better support my analysis, and was able to extrapolate extra information that previous research didn’t have.

Originally created April 2024 for ENGL 450: Modern English Grammars
